Town commands

    • /town: shows basic infomation of your town, such as your towns bank's balanace, Whome has which rank, number of plots avalable, how many bonus lots the town has.
    • /town new [name] Is used to create your town. note use '_" instead of spaces; for example /town create New_York.
    • /town deposit: is used to deosit money into your town bank, for example /town deposit 1000.
    • /town here Shortcut to the town's status of your current location.
    • /town list Shows a list of towns created.
    • /town online Lists all online resident in town
    • /town leave is to exit a town.
    • /town add: is used to members to your town, for example /town add [playername].
    • /town rank: is used to give a member a town rank such as, example /town rank add [playername] [rank].
    • /town grouplist [town] [page] Shows the names and any other infomation of the plot groups of a town.
    • /town plot Shows a detailed list of plot types of the town, and the plot types the town's residents own.
    • /town say Sends a message to all members currently online.
